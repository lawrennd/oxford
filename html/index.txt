<h1>Oxford Journal Club Examples</h1>

<p>This page describes how to use the examples in the tutorial on the GP-LVM first presented at the <a href="http://www.robots.ox.ac.uk/~cvrg/">Computer Vision Reading Group</a> at Oxford's Visual Geometry group, but later presented at several venues, see this <a href="http://www.cs.man.ac.uk/neill-bin/publications/bibpage.cgi?type=talk">Talks Page</a> for details. 

<p>For papers covering the subjects presented in the GP-LVM talks see <a href="http://www.cs.man.ac.uk/neill-bin/publications/bibpage.cgi?group=gplvm&printAbstract=1">this page</a>.


RELEASEINFORMATION

<p>Updated <code>demGplvmTalk</code> with a further stick man example for regressive dynamics. First used for MIT and Google talks.
